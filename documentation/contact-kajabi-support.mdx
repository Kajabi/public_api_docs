## How to use the conversation widget

To open the conversation widget, click the **circle** at the bottom-right of Kajabi or on the Help Center.

After you log in to Kajabi, you will be automatically authenticated in the conversation widget in Kajabi and the Help Center. This means your conversation history will be visible in both places.

<Info>
  **Notes:**

  - If you’re unable to see the conversation widget, make sure you’re not blocking cookies or using an ad blocker browser extension for Kajabi websites.
  - The conversation widget is currently not available on builder pages inside Kajabi (Pages, Checkout, Emails, Funnels).
</Info>

### Start a conversation

1. Click the **circle** at the bottom-right to open the conversation widget.
2. Click the **Messages** tab in the conversation widget to access conversations.
3. Click **Start a conversation** in the **Messages** tab.
4. Type and send a message to initiate a conversation in the conversation widget.

Please include a detailed description of what you need help with. It may be helpful to include a screenshot or video recording.

<Tip>
  **Tips:**

  - If you want to share an image, video, or file with us, simply drag and drop it into the conversation. You can also attach it from your device.
  - You can record voice messages if you prefer not to type.
</Tip>

### How the conversation widget works

The conversation widget has four tabs: **Home**, **Messages**, **Help**, and **News**.

**Home**

- **Recent message:** Quickly see the latest message from a conversation.
- **Status:** Get updates from Kajabi’s status page. This can be helpful to check if you’re experiencing an issue.
- **Resources:** Featured resources to help you grow your business on Kajabi.
- **Search for help:** View suggested articles or search Kajabi’s help articles.

**Messages**

- Click **Start a conversation** to start a new conversation. You can also view your conversation history.

**Help**

- Search or browse our help articles, right within the conversation widget.

**News**

- We’ll periodically share updates about Kajabi here, including new features or resources.

## FAQs

### What happens when I contact support?

Our AI agent will usually respond first. It’s trained on Kajabi’s help resources to help you quickly. If you prefer to talk to a person, ask to be connected to a human.

### Where do I find the conversation widget?

Click the circle at the bottom-right of Kajabi or on the Help Center.

### I can’t see the conversation widget—what should I do?

Make sure you’re not blocking cookies or using an ad blocker extension for Kajabi websites. The widget isn’t available on builder pages (Pages, Checkout, Emails, Funnels).

### Will I see my chat history in both Kajabi and the Help Center?

Yes. After you log in to Kajabi, you are automatically authenticated and your conversation history is visible in both places.

### How can I share files or voice notes?

Drag and drop images, videos, or files into the conversation, or attach them from your device. You can also record voice messages.
